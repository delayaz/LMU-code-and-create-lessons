How AI Compares Text

Only if the group has a real ~30-minute block free — otherwise this waits for Thursday morning

Before AI tools could understand meaning, one of the earliest tricks for comparing text was just counting words. It's not how modern AI works under the hood, but it's a real ancestor of the idea. We build it together, clean up the words so counting is fair, then run a group challenge to find the exact place the method breaks — which sets up a short discussion on why AI tools can be confidently wrong.
